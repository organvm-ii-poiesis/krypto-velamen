## 3. The Atomizer Engine (`services/atomizer-engine`)
The Atomizer Engine wraps the **LingFrame** framework to provide:
- **Hierarchical Decomposition**: Theme → Paragraph → Sentence → Word → Letter.
- **Particle Identification**:
    - **Hot Particles**: Words carrying high `$AFFECT_COST` or `$SIGNAL_TYPE`.
    - **Cold Particles**: Words serving as `$MASK_TYPE` or `$GENRE_ALIBI`.
- **Heuristic Scoring**: Quantifying the "Resistance" of a text based on atomic density.

## 4. In-line Atomization Syntax
Authors can now tag specific atoms within their fragments to explicitly link them to the substrate:
- **Syntax**: `[[word]]{particle: type}`
- **Example**: `The sunlight in the new place is too [[bright]]{particle: intensity}.`
