# World Research Brief: Global Queer Movements in the 2020s

## 1. Topic Scope
This brief surveys the landscape of global queer activism in the 2020s. it focuses on the fundamental fight for recognition and human rights, the rise of trans-specific movements, and the adaptation of strategies to diverse local and cultural circumstances across the globe.
