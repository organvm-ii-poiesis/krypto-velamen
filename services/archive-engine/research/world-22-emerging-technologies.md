# World Research Brief: Emerging Technologies and Queer Innovation
## Topic 22: The Future of the Instrument

---

## 1. Topic Scope
This brief explores how queer artists and technologists are utilizing emerging technologies (AI, AR, Generative Art, Blockchain) to build new forms of expression and community. It focuses on the "Integrated Workspace" as a site of innovation and the development of queer-native technology.
