# World Research Brief: Mental Health and Support Networks
## Topic 20: The Digital Sanctuary

---

## 1. Topic Scope
This brief investigates the impact of online queer communities on mental health, particularly for youth. It covers the 2020s landscape of digital interventions, serious games, and the "Necessary Safe Space" provided by social media when physical environments are hostile.
