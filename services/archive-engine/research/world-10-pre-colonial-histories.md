# World Research Brief: Pre-Colonial and Non-Western Queer Histories
## Topic 10: Gender Diversity Before the Binary

---

## 1. Topic Scope
This brief investigates the gender-diverse roles and identities that existed in global cultures prior to Western colonial influence. It covers the 15th century to the present, focusing on the suppression of these roles by colonial legal and moral regimes and their contemporary reclamation.
